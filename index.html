How to Use the System

  1. Step 1: Log In

    Visit the login page, enter your credentials, and click on the "Login" button.

  2. Step 2: Access Your Dashboard

    Once logged in, you'll be directed to the dashboard where you can access various system features.

  3. Step 3: Update Your Profile

    Navigate to the "Profile" section to update your personal information and settings.

  4. Step 4: Make a Contribution

    Go to the "Contribution" page, select the amount, and follow the instructions to complete the process.

  5. Step 5: View Reports

    After making a contribution, you can view transaction history and other reports under the "Reports" section.
